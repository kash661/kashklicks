## About the Journal

AD Photography is a solo, Toronto-based wedding and pre-wedding photography and videography studio. The journal collects the practical things couples ask before and during their shoots: where to shoot in the GTA, what to wear, how to plan timelines, and what to expect on the day. Written by Akash, who photographs every session.
