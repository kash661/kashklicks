Wedding Journey — 7 Steps + CTA
Your Journey
From Inquiry to Gallery
Step 01
Book a Consultation
A free, no-obligation call to talk about your wedding day. No pressure, just a conversation about what matters to you.
Step 02
We Plan Together
We walk through every package, answer all your questions, and find the right fit for your day and your budget.
Step 03
Lock In Your Date
Sign a simple contract and a 50% retainer secures your date. Non-refundable, because we're holding it just for you.
Step 04
Your Engagement Shoot
We book your pre-wedding session. We help pick the location, what to wear, the time of day, and the mood you're going for. This is also where we learn your editing style.
Step 05
Your Wedding Day
We capture everything. 30% is due on the day. You be fully present. We handle the memories.
Step 06
Sneak Peek
Within days of your wedding, you get a curated preview. Since we already know your style from the engagement shoot, the look is already dialed in.
Step 07
Full Delivery
Your complete gallery delivered within 2 to 3 months. Final 20% due once you're happy with everything. Private online gallery, yours to keep.
